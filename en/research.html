    • Research

    Study of exact Lagrangian submanifolds of "locally conformally symplectic" type in the cotangent bundles of closed manifolds, with canonical Liouville form. Development of tools useful to the study of those submanifolds using Morse-Novikov homology, derived sheaves theory and more differential methods.
